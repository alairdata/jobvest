Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

JobVest ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website (jobvest.vercel.app) and browser extension.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in, we collect your name and email address via Google Sign-In or email/password authentication.
  • Resume data: The resume you upload is stored securely and used solely to tailor it to job descriptions.
  • Job listing content: When you use the extension on supported job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor), we read the job description text from the current page to perform resume tailoring.
  • Application history: We store records of jobs you've applied to so you can track your progress.

How We Use Your Information

  • To tailor your resume to specific job descriptions
  • To score and analyze your resume strength
  • To sync your data across devices when you sign in
  • To track your job application history

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and on our secure servers powered by Supabase. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Used for authentication and secure data storage.
  • Google Sign-In: Used for account authentication. We only receive your name and email.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide JobVest's resume tailoring and job tracking services.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. You can delete your data at any time by signing out and clearing your browser's extension storage.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact us if you need assistance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jobvest.app@gmail.com.
